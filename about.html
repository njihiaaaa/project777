About Us

Company History

Itothya General Agencies was registered in the year 2005 and incorporated under the Companies Act on 20th June 2012. The company is a medium-sized, indigenous, locally owned and managed by Kenyans.

Our Mission

We are proud of our contribution to the development of our country's economy by scanning the financial flows of economic agents. We differ from our competitors by our friendly approach to debtors who are unable to pay.

Our Vision

To be a leading service provider in debt collection and investigations for all major corporate clients in the country.

Our Values
  • Clients: The utmost satisfaction of our clients is our supreme aim.
  • Employees: We invest in training and ensure good working conditions.
  • Professional Ethics: We respect the law and maintain confidentiality.
  • Professionalism: We strive for continuous improvement and innovation.
What Drives Our Business

We have developed clear strategies to use our skills and understanding of clients to deliver unique products and services at competitive rates.
